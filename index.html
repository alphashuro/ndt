Heat Treatment

As a result of our Heat Treatment expertise, we are able to offer the industry a host of heat treatment processes, designed to obtain the most out of metals and alloys, particularly steel. All equipment inspected prior to work commencement. Temperature charts are fully identified and referenced with respect to the thermocouple/ trace arrangements. The charts also identify the heat treatment temperatures, date(s) and time(s), and are accompanied by reports completed and signed off by a heat treatment technician..

Sand blasting and Painting

Our Sand Blast & Paint (Corrosion Protection) department delivers a full service to various clients in various markets, including the light and heavy steel industry. Our fully qualified and experienced staff ensures that work is done to the client specifications and delivered on time.
NDT Projects boasts a Sandblasting yard in Vanderbijlpark, capable of handling: Structural Steel, Process Piping to Material Handling equipment such as Ship Loaders, Drum Reclaimers & Stackers, Conveyor Systems, and Process Equipment including Pressure Vessels.
We work with our clients to specify the best coating system for the application and environment. NDT Projects additionally provide Pickling & Passivation.

Safety

At NDT Projects our constant goal is to ensure zero injuries to anyone, anytime. We work hard to improve our best practice global safety systems and processes as well as strengthen the knowledge and skills of our team members. These efforts are reflected in our reputation for delivering projects with world class safety conditions

Health, Safety and Environmental Mission:

NDT Projects is dedicated to providing a safe and healthful environment for our employees and our customer’s employees; protecting the assets and property of our customers and the public while also preserving our environment.
Safety is a Team Goal. Our most valuable resources are the people who work for us and the customers we serve. To prevent injuries and damages to property, we will comply with all government regulations and national/international codes particularly those applicable to safety, health and environmental issues
We want all of our people to be safety coaches, bringing out the best in one another and encouraging each other in efforts that maintain an accident free record.
We recognize that NDT technicians are often closest to the risk and we will therefore ensure that our greatest efforts and energies go toward keeping them safe and free from harm to health.
